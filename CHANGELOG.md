# Change Log

## [2.0.0](https://github.com/HuntsmanCancerInstitute-RISR/hci-ng-grid/tree/v2.0.0)
    - Refactor of rendering and how data/config/events are listened to.
    - Listeners for saving cells and rows.

    - Numerous fixes.
